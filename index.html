EDI & Technology
Reading Group
Equity in the Digital Divide, AI, and More
When:
Where:
Mondays, 1pm

Who to Contact:
david.thomas.7@bc.edu (Dave Thomas)
Description:
We discuss a range of readings on EDI issues and technology... from the potential and the dangers of “AI” and data analytics... to how even systems like Pokémon GO reflect and reinforce structural inequality.

Readings do not carry over from week to week, so you are free to join, leave, or drop in, at any time! If you have suggested readings, we’d love to hear!
